## R0 (Decacorn) review
Google would not ship a "full ledger export" that reads bounded DB pages but then stores the entire result and complete CSV in application memory. The endpoint must provide truthful UX and a performance budget: either stream the full ledger, expose an explicit asynchronous export job, or document and enforce a visible cap. The hidden 100k cap is gone, but the replacement still fails the Google-scale reliability bar because million-row exports can OOM before completing.
