# Product Dominance Playbook — Digest (operator side-read, 2026-05-28)

Strategic frame for all TGP product work. Source: product-dominance-playbook.docx.

## Core thesis
Great product = the CEILING (retention/word-of-mouth). Distribution = the ENGINE (speed).
Need BOTH simultaneously (Bezos/Thiel). Most great products die on distribution; most great
distribution dies on retention (=product).

## 8 Killers (failure modes to design against)
1. **Inertia Wall / switching cost** — "Better" isn't enough. Need <30-min Trainerize/Everfit migration
   (one-click CSV import, "Switch from X" LP, migration guarantee). Google+ lesson: network effects beat features.
2. **Solving the wrong problem elegantly** — solve FELT pain first: chasing payment, embarrassing branding,
   high SaaS fees, clients quitting after week 2. These must be the FIRST things a new user experiences.
3. **No single distribution channel nailed** — pick ONE (TGP = SMMA content → free-app CTA). Engineer it as a machine.
4. **Onboarding that kills activation** — D1 retention industry avg 21-25%. Interactive first-use (do, don't watch)
   took one app 21%→44%. Funnel everything to the aha moment.
5. **Feature creep destroying the UX moat** — launch Apple-simple, 18mo later 3 nav bars + 40 settings. Defenses:
   4-question gate per feature, "one in/one out", quarterly click-count audits, design veto, complexity budget/screen.
6. **Pricing psychology** — free hook can signal low value; 3.6% model only "feels" cheaper when billing >$2k/mo.
7. **Trust gap for payment infra** — must be bulletproof BEFORE marketing. One payout failure = Twitter thread.
8. **Founder-market fit erosion** — support load + 2 skillsets (product + distribution) + runway.

## Aha moment (activation metric)
Candidates: **"assigned first program to a client"** (program-delivery aha), "first payment collected",
"client completed first workout". The Master Workout Builder's assign→client-visibility path IS the
activation-critical flow. Build to make that moment happen FAST.

## 10x features (focus engineering here)
Free pricing · auto failed-payment recovery · revenue split/payroll · white label ·
**AI program builder (hours→minutes)**. NOT 10x: marginally better exercise library, faster loading.

## Competitive position (vs Trainerize / Everfit)
TGP: $0/mo forever, 3.6% all-in, unlimited clients, Apple-level UX, 18 billing features,
AI (form check, program builder, adaptive training), revenue split/payroll, SMMA content moat,
white-label included, corporate wellness billing. Competitors structurally locked out of free+transaction model.

## Implications for the Master Workout Builder (TO-DO #1)
- AI "paste text/notes → instant trackable workout in seconds" is BOTH the #B requirement AND the
  Killer-#1 migration wedge (Everfit's exact pitch). Treat free-text/paste import as first-class AI input.
- Hold the UX moat (Killer #5): complexity budget per screen, progressive disclosure (Design Bible 20% rule),
  ≤5 primary actions, smart defaults. The builder must feel Apple-simple despite Everfit-plus power.
- Assign→client-visibility is the activation aha — must be instant, reliable, persisted, and visible to client (R-grade).
- Notifications as product surface: "Your program for tomorrow is ready" (client), "client hasn't logged in 3 days" (coach).
- Push opt-in only AFTER value (post first-assignment / first-payment), never on first launch.
